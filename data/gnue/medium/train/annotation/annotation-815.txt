<topic>Forms</topic>

<p>John Lenton (Chipaca) asked <quote who="John Lenton">how do 
I get a tooltip?</quote>. Derek Neighbors (dneighbo) said it used 
the options tag, and gave an example: 
<quote who="Derek Neighbors">
&lt;entry name="text2" x="1" y="5" width="10"&gt; 
   &lt;options&gt; 
      &lt;option name="tip" value="This is the tip for the second text box"/&gt; 
   &lt;/options&gt; 
&lt;/entry&gt;
</quote>.</p>
